CENTER FOR VERNACULAR ARCHITECTURE TRUST

JULY - DEC 2020

Internship

  • The CENTER FOR VERNACULAR ARCHITECTURE TRUST has grown out of Shramik, a co-operative of building craftsmen initiated by the late R. L. Kumar, in the late 80’ s as part of his work with the CIEDS Collective.
  • Vernacular Architecture is a little known and less explored field that is concerned with holistic architectural building traditions / practices that are cost effective, ecologically sensitive and culturally relevant. This internship period had me concentrated on learning, implementing and executing the technical aspects of s designed space.
